Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really a one-time payment? ▾
Yes. You pay once and own EvoFit Meals forever. No monthly fees, no annual renewal, no per-client charges. If you take on 50 new clients next month, it costs you nothing extra.
How does AI meal plan generation work? ▾
You describe your client in plain English — their dietary protocol, calorie target, restrictions, and goals. EvoFit Meals sends that to the AI generator and returns a complete 7-day plan with recipes, macros, and a grocery list. It takes about 60 seconds.
Can my clients access their plans directly? ▾
Yes. Every plan gets a shareable client link — no account required on their end. They open the link and see their full meal plan, grocery list, and macros. On Professional and Enterprise, the link is branded with your logo.
What dietary protocols are supported? ▾
EvoFit Meals supports 8+ protocols including keto, paleo, Mediterranean, vegan, vegetarian, high-protein, low-carb, and balanced macros. You can also mix and customize — for example "Mediterranean, high-protein, no shellfish."
Can I upgrade from Starter to Professional later? ▾
Yes. Upgrades cost the difference between tiers — Starter to Professional is $100, Professional to Enterprise is $100. All your clients, plans, and history carry over automatically.
