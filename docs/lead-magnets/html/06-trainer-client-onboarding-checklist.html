First 30 Days Playbook

Follow this week-by-week plan to deliver results and lock in long-term clients.

Week 1 — Baseline Assessment

Understand Where They Are

  • Review completed intake form and identify key dietary patterns
  • Calculate TDEE and set macro targets based on their goal
  • Build and deliver their first personalized 7-day meal plan
  • Include a grocery list and 2–3 meal prep suggestions
  • Schedule a 15-minute kickoff call to walk through the plan
  • Ask them to log meals for 3 days so you can see real eating habits
Week 2 — First Plan Delivered & Active

Build the Habit

  • Check in via text/message: "How's the first week going? Any recipes you loved?"
  • Review their food log and identify any compliance issues
  • Make small adjustments: swap disliked meals, adjust portion sizes
  • Deliver Week 2 plan with 2–3 new recipes to keep it fresh
  • Share one quick nutrition tip relevant to their goal (e.g., protein timing)
  • Celebrate any early wins — even small ones ("Great job hitting protein 5/7 days!")
Week 3 — Check-In & Adjust

Refine and Optimize

  • Conduct a formal check-in: weight, measurements, energy, sleep, hunger levels
  • Adjust macros if needed (e.g., increase carbs on training days)
  • Address any challenges: dining out, social events, cravings
  • Provide a "dining out cheat sheet" for their favorite restaurants
  • Deliver Week 3 plan with increased variety based on feedback
  • Ask: "What's been the hardest part?" — solve it proactively
Week 4 — Progress Review & Upsell

Show Results, Secure Retention

  • Full progress review: compare Week 1 vs. Week 4 metrics
  • Create a visual progress summary (before/after stats, compliance %)
  • Deliver Month 2 plan with new recipes and any macro adjustments
  • Present continuation options: "Based on your progress, here's what Month 2 looks like"
  • Ask for a testimonial: "Would you mind sharing a quick quote about your experience?"
  • Offer a referral incentive: "Know a friend who'd benefit? They get 10% off, you get a free week"

The Retention Secret

Clients don't leave because of bad plans — they leave because they feel forgotten. The check-ins in this playbook are more important than the plans themselves. A 30-second "How's it going?" text on Wednesday costs you nothing and keeps clients for months longer.
