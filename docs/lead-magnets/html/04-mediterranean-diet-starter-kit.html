The Mediterranean Pyramid

A visual guide to balancing your plate. The base forms your daily foundation; the top is for occasional enjoyment.

🍦 🥩 Red Meat & Sweets Beef, lamb, pastries, sugar Monthly or less
🥚 🍳 🧀 Poultry, Eggs & Cheese Chicken, turkey, yogurt, feta, eggs A few times per week
🐟 🦐 🦍 Fish & Seafood Salmon, sardines, shrimp, tuna, mussels 2–3 times per week
🥤 🥦 🍂 🥑 Olive Oil, Legumes, Nuts & Seeds Extra-virgin olive oil, lentils, chickpeas, almonds, walnuts Daily
🥦 🍅 🍎 🍛 🥐 Vegetables, Fruits & Whole Grains Tomatoes, spinach, berries, farro, brown rice, whole wheat bread Every meal — Foundation of the diet

🥤 Healthy Fats

  • 2–4 tbsp olive oil daily
  • 1 handful nuts (1 oz)
  • Half an avocado

🍎 Fruits & Vegetables

  • 5–9 servings daily
  • 1 serving = 1 cup raw
  • Variety of colors

🍛 Whole Grains

  • 3–6 servings daily
  • 1 serving = 1/2 cup cooked
  • Choose minimally processed

🐟 Protein Sources

  • Fish 2–3x per week (4–6 oz)
  • Legumes daily (1/2 cup)
  • Poultry 2x per week

Pantry Essentials

Stock these staples and you'll always be ready to cook a Mediterranean meal in under 30 minutes.

Herbs & Spices

Fresh Produce (Weekly)

💡 Pro Tip: The Mediterranean Flavor Base

Keep these three always on hand: extra-virgin olive oil, garlic, and lemons. With just these three ingredients plus salt, you can elevate any vegetable, grain, or protein into a Mediterranean-style dish in minutes.
