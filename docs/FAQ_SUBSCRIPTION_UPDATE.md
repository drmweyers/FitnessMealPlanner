## One-Time Payments

### How do one-time payments work?

1. **Pay once** for your selected tier (Starter, Professional, or Enterprise)
2. **Get lifetime access** to the platform
3. **Generate limited meal plans** each month (20, 50, or 150 depending on tier)
4. **No recurring charges** ever

### What are the monthly limits?

| Tier | Monthly Meal Plans |
|------|-------------------|
| Starter | 20 meal plans/month |
| Professional | 50 meal plans/month |
| Enterprise | 150 meal plans/month |

### When do my meal plan credits reset?

Your usage counter resets on the **1st of every month at midnight** (server time).

**Example:**
- Jan 15: Use 18/20 meal plans
- Feb 1 at 00:00: Counter resets to 0/20
- Feb 1 at 00:01: You have 20 meal plans available again

### What happens when I hit my limit?

When you reach your monthly limit, you have two options:

**Option 1: Wait for reset (FREE)**
- Your counter automatically resets on the 1st of next month
- You'll receive [Monthly Limit] meal plans again

**Option 2: Upgrade to subscription (RECOMMENDED)**
- Get unlimited meal plan generation immediately
- No more waiting for monthly resets
- Cancel anytime

### Can I purchase additional meal plans?

Not currently. You can either:
1. Wait until the 1st of next month (counter resets)
2. Upgrade to a subscription plan for unlimited access

## Usage Limits

### How do I check my current usage?

**View your usage in two places:**

1. **Usage Dashboard:** [evofitmeals.com/usage](https://evofitmeals.com/usage)
   - Real-time usage statistics
   - Visual progress bar
   - Days until reset
   - Upgrade options

2. **Trainer Dashboard:** Top-right corner shows your current usage

### Will I be notified when I'm close to my limit?

**Yes!** You'll receive two email notifications:

1. **80% Warning:** "You're approaching your monthly limit"
   - Sent when you've used 80% of your monthly allocation
   - Gives you time to plan or upgrade

2. **100% Limit Reached:** "You've reached your monthly limit"
   - Sent when you hit your limit

### What happens if I reach my limit mid-month?

If you reach your limit before the month ends:

1. **You'll see a usage limit message** when trying to generate meal plans

3. **You cannot generate new meal plans** until:
   - The 1st of next month (counter resets), OR
   - You upgrade to a subscription plan

### Do subscriptions have usage limits?

**No!** Subscription plans include **UNLIMITED** meal plan generation. No counters, no limits, no worries.

### What counts toward my usage limit?

Only **new meal plan generation** counts toward your limit:

**Counts toward limit:**
- âœ… Generating a new meal plan (AI or manual)
- âœ… Creating a meal plan from scratch

**Does NOT count toward limit:**
- âŒ Viewing existing meal plans

## Upgrades & Downgrades

### How do I upgrade from one-time payment to subscription?

1. Go to **[Pricing Page](https://evofitmeals.com/pricing?upgrade=true)**
2. Click **"Upgrade to Subscription"** on your desired tier
3. Enter payment information
4. Click **"Start Subscription"**

**What happens:**
- âœ… Unlimited meal plan generation starts immediately
- âœ… Your one-time purchase remains valid (you can revert anytime)
- âœ… Monthly billing starts on the day you upgrade

### Can I upgrade to a higher subscription tier?

**Yes!** You can upgrade at any time:

1. Go to **[Billing Settings](https://evofitmeals.com/billing/change-plan)**
2. Select your new tier (Professional or Enterprise)
3. Click **"Upgrade"**

**Billing:**
- You'll be charged the **prorated difference** immediately
- Your billing date remains the same
- Next month, you'll be charged the new tier's full price

**Example:**
- Current: Starter ($19/month), 15 days into billing cycle
- Upgrade to: Professional ($49/month)
- Prorated charge: ~$15 (half of $30 difference)
- Next billing: $49 on your normal billing date

### Can I downgrade my subscription tier?

**Yes!** You can downgrade at any time:

1. Go to **[Billing Settings](https://evofitmeals.com/billing/change-plan)**
2. Select your new tier (Starter or Professional)
3. Click **"Downgrade"**

**Billing:**
- Downgrade takes effect at the **end of your current billing period**
- You keep your current tier's benefits until then
- Next month, you'll be charged the lower tier's price

**Example:**
- Current: Enterprise ($99/month), billing renews Feb 15
- Downgrade to: Professional ($49/month) on Jan 20
- Jan 20 - Feb 15: Still have Enterprise access
- Feb 15: Charged $49, now on Professional plan

### What happens to my one-time payment if I subscribe?

**Your one-time payment stays valid!**

If you cancel your subscription later:
- âœ… You automatically revert to your one-time payment plan
- âœ… Monthly usage limits apply again (20, 50, or 150 depending on tier)
- âœ… No additional purchase needed

**Think of it as two separate plans:**
- **Plan A:** One-time payment (always available)
- **Plan B:** Subscription (optional upgrade)

You can switch between them anytime.

## Cancellations & Refunds

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to **[Billing Settings](https://evofitmeals.com/billing/cancel)**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. You'll receive a confirmation email

### When does my cancellation take effect?

**You keep access until the end of your current billing period.**

**Example:**
- Subscription renews on the 15th of each month
- You cancel on Jan 10
- You have access until Feb 15 (end of current period)
- No charge on Feb 15
- Access reverts to one-time payment plan after Feb 15

**You're not charged for the next month.**

### What happens after I cancel?

After your current billing period ends:

1. **Subscription ends** (no more charges)
2. **Account reverts** to your one-time payment plan
3. **Usage limits apply** again (20, 50, or 150 per month)
4. **All your data stays intact** (meal plans, clients, recipes, etc.)

**Nothing is deleted** - you just have limited monthly meal plan generation instead of unlimited.

### Can I reactivate my subscription?

**Yes!** You can reactivate anytime:

1. Go to **[Billing Settings](https://evofitmeals.com/billing/reactivate)**
2. Click **"Reactivate Subscription"**
3. Confirm billing information
4. Unlimited access resumes immediately

### Are refunds available?

**Subscription payments:**
- âŒ No refunds on monthly subscription charges
- âœ… You keep access for the full billing period you paid for
- âœ… Cancel anytime to avoid future charges

**One-time payments:**
- âœ… 30-day money-back guarantee

### What if I cancel by mistake?

If you cancel your subscription accidentally:

Contact **support@evofitmeals.com** if you need help.
