# EvoFitMeals vs. Other Meal Planning Apps

Who this is for
- Coaches evaluating platforms for speed, scale, and personalization

Head-to-head criteria
- Recipe generation scale and speed
- Personalization depth (protocols, exclusions, macro control)
- Client capacity and management
- Export quality and branding
- Ease of onboarding and ongoing workflows

How EvoFitMeals stands out
- 500+ recipe options in ~60 seconds per generation
- 8+ dietary protocols with macro balancing by goal
- Scales to 200+ clients with client management and notes
- Professional branded PDF exports clients actually use
- Clear onboarding with templates and checklists

When another app might fit
- You need deep grocery delivery integrations today
- You require a specific EHR/CRM integration not yet supported
- Your use case is general wellness content vs. performance nutrition

Evaluation checklist
- List must-have protocols and exclusions
- Time a full plan creation end-to-end
- Review export quality with your branding
- Test plan edits and partial regeneration
- Confirm capacity needs and pricing fit

Notes
- Always verify current integrations and roadmap with sales; features evolve quickly.
