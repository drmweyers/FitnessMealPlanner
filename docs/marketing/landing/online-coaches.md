1) Invite clients via email — they create accounts and set up profiles
2) Generate meal plans with AI or create manually — 8+ dietary protocols supported
3) Assign plans, share via link, and track progress with measurements and photos

Core capabilities
- AI-powered recipe generation with macro targeting and dietary protocol support
- Natural language input ("high protein vegetarian, 1800 cal, no soy")
- Auto-generated grocery lists from any meal plan
- Shareable meal plan links (public access with token — no client login required)
- Client progress tracking with body measurements and progress photos

FAQ
- Can I deliver plans digitally? Yes — branded PDF export and shareable links. Clients can view shared plans without logging in.
- How do I keep clients engaged? Assign new plans regularly, track their progress measurements, and use variety across dietary protocols.
- What if clients dislike certain ingredients? Ingredient exclusion lists are respected by the AI generator.
- Can I combine protocols (e.g., high-protein vegetarian)? Yes — multiple protocols and macro targets can be combined.
- Do I need a native app? No — the platform is web-based and works on any device with a browser.
