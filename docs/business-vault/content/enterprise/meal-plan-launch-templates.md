# Meal Plan Launch Templates

## 12 Launch Sequences for Nutrition Challenges, Seasonal Plans, Group Programs, Corporate Wellness, and Subscription Services

---

### How to Use This Guide

Every successful nutrition program launch follows the same arc: build anticipation, create urgency, deliver an exceptional experience, and convert participants into ongoing clients. This guide gives you 12 complete launch sequences — each with pre-launch content, launch-day messaging, mid-campaign touchpoints, and post-launch conversion templates.

Don't run all 12 at once. Pick the one that matches your current goal, execute it fully, and move on to the next. Most enterprise nutrition programs run 4-6 launches per year — one per quarter with bonus seasonal launches.

**For team-based operations:** Assign each launch to a lead coach. The lead coach owns the content calendar, messaging, and participant management. The program director oversees the overall launch calendar and ensures launches don't cannibalize each other.

## Launch 1: The 4-Week Nutrition Challenge

**Best for:** Lead generation, community building, and converting challengers into monthly clients.

**Pre-Launch (10 days before):**

_Day -10: Announcement_

> Subject: The [Challenge Name] is coming — [Date]
>
> Something big is happening in [X] days.
>
> I'm launching a 4-week Nutrition Challenge designed to help you [specific outcome — lose your first 5 lbs / build a meal prep habit / eat clean for 28 days straight]. It's not a crash diet. It's not a detox. It's a structured challenge with a custom meal plan, weekly group coaching, and a community of people doing it alongside you.
>
> Details drop on [date]. Reply "IN" if you want first access.

_Day -7: Details + Early Registration_

> Here's the full breakdown of the [Challenge Name]:
>
> **What you get:**
>
> - 4-week customized meal plan (built in EvoFit Meals, tailored to your goals)
> - Weekly group coaching call (live, with Q&A)
> - Private community group for daily check-ins and support
> - Progress tracking template
> - Grocery lists and prep guides every week
>
> **The commitment:**
>
> - Follow your plan for 28 days
> - Check in daily in the community
> - Show up to weekly coaching calls
>
> **The investment:** $[X] for the full 4 weeks
>
> **Early bird:** Register before [date] and save $[X].
>
> [Registration link]

_Day -3: Social Proof + Final Push_

> "Last time I ran this challenge, [X] people joined. Here's what happened: [specific results — '12 participants lost a combined 87 lbs' / 'Average adherence was 82%' / '8 of the 15 participants went on to become monthly clients']. This round has [X] spots. We start [date]."

**Launch Day:**

> "We're live. The [Challenge Name] starts today. If you registered, your meal plan is in your dashboard. If you haven't registered yet, there are [X] spots left. Join before midnight: [link]"

**Mid-Challenge (Week 2):**

> "Two weeks in. Here's what I'm seeing: [specific observations — 'Protein compliance is up across the group' / 'The Tuesday stir-fry recipe is the group favorite' / '3 people hit their first milestone this week']. If you're struggling, bring it to Wednesday's call. That's what the group is for."

**Post-Challenge Conversion:**

> "28 days. Done. Here's your results summary: [group-level stats]. Now — what happens next matters more than what happened during the challenge. The habits you built over 4 weeks don't sustain themselves without structure. That's why I'm offering challenge participants a seamless transition to my monthly nutrition coaching at $[X]/month — $[X] less than the standard rate, locked in for 6 months. Your plan continues. Your progress continues. Reply to this message to lock it in."

## Launch 8: Launch Checklist (Universal)

**Use this checklist for every launch. Print it. Check every box.**

**4 Weeks Before:**

- [ ] Define the program: name, duration, price, what's included
- [ ] Build 3 sample meal plans that represent the program
- [ ] Write all email copy (announcement, details, urgency, launch day, follow-up)
- [ ] Create social media content for 7-day campaign
- [ ] Set up registration page with payment processing
- [ ] Decide on participant cap (scarcity drives action)

**2 Weeks Before:**

- [ ] Send announcement email to full list
- [ ] Post teaser content on social media
- [ ] Personally message your top 20 prospects
- [ ] Brief your coaching team on the launch (for team operations)
- [ ] Prepare onboarding materials (questionnaires, welcome emails, community setup)

**1 Week Before:**

- [ ] Send details + early bird email
- [ ] Run social media campaign (Days 1-5)
- [ ] Follow up with anyone who expressed interest but hasn't registered
- [ ] Test the registration and payment flow yourself

**Launch Day:**

- [ ] Send launch email
- [ ] Post on all social platforms
- [ ] Deliver first plans to all registered participants
- [ ] Send welcome message and community invitation
- [ ] Activate the onboarding drip sequence

**Post-Launch (Week 1):**

- [ ] Check in with every participant by Day 3
- [ ] Address any plan issues or swaps immediately
- [ ] Post first content in the community group
- [ ] Follow up with registrants who haven't accessed their plan

## Launch 9: Beta Tester Framework

**For launching a new program, recipe category, or feature — use beta testers to validate before a public launch.**

**How to select beta testers:**

- 5-10 of your most engaged, honest clients
- Mix of experience levels (new and long-tenured)
- People who will actually provide feedback, not just enjoy a free service

**The Beta Offer:**

> "[Name], I'm developing a new [program/feature] and I want your honest feedback before I launch it publicly. I'm selecting 5-10 clients to test it for [2-4 weeks] at no additional cost. In exchange, I need: weekly feedback on what works and what doesn't, a candid assessment at the end, and permission to use your experience in launch materials (anonymously if preferred). Interested?"

**Beta Feedback Template (send weekly):**

1. On a scale of 1-10, how useful was this week's [plan/feature]?
2. What was the best part?
3. What was the most frustrating part?
4. What would you change?
5. Would you recommend this to another client?

**Using Beta Results for Launch:**

> "Before launching [Program Name] publicly, I tested it with 8 existing clients for 4 weeks. Here's what happened: [results — adherence rates, satisfaction scores, specific quotes]. This isn't hypothetical. This is proven."

## Launch 10: Group Nutrition Challenge Blueprint

**A complete framework for running a group nutrition challenge as a recurring revenue and lead generation tool.**

**Challenge Structure:**

| Element     | Detail                                                               |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Duration    | 4 weeks (sweet spot for commitment and results)                      |
| Group size  | 10-25 participants (enough for community, small enough for coaching) |
| Pricing     | $97-197 per participant                                              |
| Frequency   | Run quarterly (Jan, Apr, Jul, Oct)                                   |
| Coach ratio | 1 coach per 15 participants                                          |

**Weekly Schedule:**

| Day       | Activity                                              |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Monday    | New weekly plan delivered + weekly theme announced    |
| Wednesday | Live group coaching call (30 min)                     |
| Friday    | Community celebration: share your best meal this week |
| Daily     | Coach posts one piece of content in community         |
| Daily     | Participants check in with adherence (1-5 score)      |

**Scoring System:**

| Action                                    | Points        |
| ----------------------------------------- | ------------- |
| Daily check-in posted                     | 1 point/day   |
| Followed plan 80%+ (self-reported weekly) | 5 points/week |
| Attended group coaching call              | 3 points/call |
| Shared a recipe or tip in the community   | 2 points each |
| Referred a friend who joins               | 10 points     |

**Prizes:**

- 1st place: Free 3 months of monthly nutrition coaching
- 2nd place: Free 1 month of monthly nutrition coaching
- 3rd place: $50 gift card

**Post-Challenge Conversion Offer:**

> "The challenge is over. The habits are built. Now the question: do you want to keep this going or go back to guessing? Challenge participants get exclusive access to my monthly coaching at $[X]/month — that's $[X] below my standard rate. Your plan continues seamlessly. Your coach stays the same. The community stays active. Reply 'YES' and I'll lock you in."

**Expected conversion:** 40-60% of challenge participants convert to monthly clients if the challenge experience was strong.

## Launch 11: Corporate Wellness Launch Sequence

>
> What this means for you:
>
> - A customized meal plan designed around your goals, preferences, and schedule
> - Monthly check-ins with a dedicated nutrition coach

>
> Enrollment opens [date] and closes [date]. Spots are limited to [X] employees for this initial launch.
>
> [Enrollment link]

**Week 2: Information Session**

Host a 30-minute virtual lunch-and-learn for interested employees:

1. Why nutrition matters for energy, productivity, and health (10 min)
2. How the program works — walkthrough of the platform and coaching process (10 min)
3. Q&A (10 min)
4. How to enroll (live enrollment link shared during session)

> "Enrollment for our nutrition coaching benefit closes this Friday. [X] of [Y] spots are filled. If you've been thinking about it, now's the time: [link]"

**Week 4: First Plans Delivered**

> "Your personalized meal plan is live. Access it here: [link]. Your nutrition coach [Name] will reach out within 24 hours to schedule your first check-in."

**Quarterly Report to HR:**

> "[Company] Nutrition Program — Q[X] Results:
>

> - Active participation rate: [X]%
> - Average satisfaction score: [X]/5
> - Top result: [anonymized — 'One participant reported 15% increase in daily energy levels']

## Launch 12: Seasonal Subscription Launch (Fall/Winter Edition)

**A complete launch sequence for a seasonal meal plan subscription — runs October through February.**

**Theme:** Comfort food that hits your macros. Warm, filling, family-friendly meals that prove you don't need to eat cold salads to stay on track during the colder months.

**Pre-Launch Email:**

> "Let me guess: every fall, your nutrition falls apart. The salads stop. The soups are heavy. The holidays hit and suddenly it's January and you're starting over."
>
> "What if this year was different? I'm launching the Fall/Winter Nutrition Subscription — 5 months of seasonal meal plans designed for comfort food lovers who still want results. Slow-cooker meals, hearty stews, warm grain bowls, and holiday-proof plans that keep you on track through Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year's."
>
> "Subscribe: $[X]/month for 5 months (October-February). First plan delivered October 1st."

**Monthly Plan Themes:**

| Month    | Theme           | Recipe Focus                                                          |
| -------- | --------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| October  | Harvest Comfort | Root vegetables, squash, slow-cooker proteins                         |
| November | Holiday Prep    | Thanksgiving-friendly recipes, hosting plans, travel meals            |
| December | Festive Fuel    | Party-season strategies, celebration meals, cookie-season survival    |
| January  | Fresh Start     | Lighter comfort food, reset meals, New Year energy                    |
| February | Heart-Healthy   | Omega-3 rich meals, anti-inflammatory focus, Valentine's dinner plans |

**Mid-Subscription Retention Message (December):**

> "You're 3 months into the Fall/Winter subscription and here's what I'm seeing: your adherence through the holiday season was [X]% — that's [above average / impressive / something we'll build on]. Two months remain. January and February are where the real magic happens — you'll come out the other side with habits that outlast winter. Keep going."
