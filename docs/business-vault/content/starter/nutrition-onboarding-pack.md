# Nutrition Client Onboarding Pack

## Dietary Questionnaires, Food Preference Surveys, Allergy Forms, and Welcome Sequences

---

### What This Is

This is everything you need to onboard a new nutrition client professionally. Copy the templates, customize them for your brand, and use them every time a new client signs up. A structured onboarding process does three things: it collects the information you need to build an accurate plan, it sets expectations so the client knows exactly what to expect, and it signals professionalism that justifies your pricing.

Each template in this pack is ready to use. Adjust the wording to match your voice, add your branding, and send them out.

---

**What to Expect: Your Nutrition Service Guide**

**Welcome.** This guide tells you everything you need to know about how your nutrition service works, what you'll receive, and how to get the most out of it.

---

| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |

| Recipes with instructions | Included in every plan                      | Step-by-step with portions                |
| Macro/calorie breakdown   | Every meal, every day                       | Built into your plan                      |
| Shopping list             | Every plan cycle                            | Consolidated and organized by section     |
| Nutrition check-in        | Monthly (or as per your package)            | Video call, phone, or in-person           |
| Plan adjustments          | As needed based on progress                 | Requested through [your contact method]   |

---

**How Your Plan Is Built**

Your meal plan is created using EvoFit Meals, a professional nutrition platform. Here's the process:

1. I review your questionnaire, preferences, and restrictions
2. I calculate your calorie and macro targets based on your goals, activity level, and body composition
3. I generate a plan that matches your preferences, cooking skills, and schedule
4. I review every plan personally before it reaches you — no generic output
5. You receive the plan with full recipes, portions, and a shopping list

If something doesn't work — a recipe you hate, an ingredient you can't find, a day that doesn't fit your schedule — tell me and I'll adjust it. The plan should serve you, not the other way around.

---

**How to Get the Best Results**

1. **Follow the plan for at least 5 full days before requesting changes.** Give your body and your routine a chance to adapt. Minor discomfort in the first few days (cravings, adjustment to new foods) is normal.

2. **Prep what you can.** You don't need to prep every meal, but having 2-3 days of lunches ready to go dramatically improves adherence. Your plan includes prep-friendly meals for this reason.

3. **Track your intake for the first 2 weeks.** Even rough tracking helps you build awareness of portions. After 2 weeks, most clients can eyeball portions accurately.

4. **Communicate honestly.** If you skip meals, eat off-plan, or have a rough week — tell me. I'm not here to judge. I'm here to adjust. A plan that accounts for real life works better than a perfect plan you can't follow.

5. **Ask questions.** There are no dumb questions in nutrition. If you don't understand why a food is in your plan, or you want to swap something, or you're confused about a recipe — ask.

---

**What to Do If...**

| Situation                    | What to Do                                                                                                                     |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| You hate a recipe            | Message me — I'll swap it within 24 hours                                                                                      |
| You can't find an ingredient | Substitute with something similar or message me for an alternative                                                             |
| You're eating out            | Choose the closest match from your plan's macros. A photo of the menu helps — send it and I'll suggest the best option         |
| You're traveling             | Let me know 5+ days in advance and I'll build a travel-friendly version                                                        |
| You're sick                  | Eat what you can. Don't stress the plan. Let me know when you're back and we'll reset                                          |
| You hit a plateau            | Bring it up at our check-in — I'll review and adjust targets                                                                   |
| You want to stop             | No hard feelings — but please let me know rather than going quiet. If something isn't working, I'd rather fix it than lose you |

---

**Check-In Protocol**

Your check-in is your chance to tell me what's working, what isn't, and what's changed. Come prepared with:

1. Your weight trend for the past week(s) — morning weigh-ins preferred
2. How closely you followed the plan (percentage is fine: "I was about 80% on plan")
3. Energy levels, sleep quality, and how you feel overall
4. Any foods or meals that need swapping
5. Any upcoming schedule changes (travel, events, holidays)

I'll use this information to adjust your plan for the next cycle.

---

**Cancellation and Pauses**

- To cancel: Provide [X days] notice before your next billing cycle
- To pause: I offer [X]-month pauses for travel, injury, or life events — just let me know
- Re-engagement: If you cancel and want to come back, your previous plan and preferences are saved in EvoFit Meals. We can pick up where you left off

---

**Scope Reminder**

Your meal plans are designed for general health, fitness performance, and body composition goals. If you have a medical condition that requires dietary management (diabetes, eating disorders, kidney disease, autoimmune conditions, etc.), I recommend working with a registered dietitian alongside our work together. I'm happy to coordinate with your healthcare team to ensure your plan supports their recommendations.
