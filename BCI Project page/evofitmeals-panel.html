EvoFit Meals

AI-powered meal planning for fitness trainers. Pay once, plan forever.

Built by BCI students to solve a real problem: fitness trainers spending 3-5 hours/week on manual meal planning. EvoFit Meals uses GPT-4 to generate personalized meal plans in plain English, manage client nutrition, and export branded PDFs — all with a one-time payment model that saves trainers $2,000+ vs subscription competitors. Now serving real trainers in production with 4,000+ AI-generated recipes across 8+ dietary protocols.

Key Features

  • AI meal plan generation from natural language — describe requirements in plain English
  • 4,000+ recipes across 8+ dietary protocols (keto, vegan, paleo, halal, and more)
  • Multi-client management with invitation system, bulk assignments, and progress tracking
  • Branded PDF exports, shareable meal plan links, and auto-generated grocery lists
  • 3-tier pricing (Starter/Professional/Enterprise) with Stripe payments and custom branding
  • Production-grade: 1,510+ tests, 95% coverage, RBAC, OAuth, and full API
